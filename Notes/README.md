# 📓 Notes Directory

Welcome to the Notes directory!

## How to use this directory:

1. Create Markdown (`.md`) or text files to jot down important concepts, interview points, and summaries from your daily study sessions.
2. Copy the "Daily Notes" section from each daily plan and expand on them with your own understanding.
3. Write down common pitfalls, errors you encountered, and how you fixed them.

**Example Structure:**
```
Notes/
├── OOP-Concepts.md
├── Important-String-Methods.md
├── Multithreading-Cheat-Sheet.md
└── Common-Exceptions.md
```

**Why keep notes?**
Writing down concepts in your own words is one of the best ways to solidify your understanding and makes for quick revision before your company training assessments and future interviews.
